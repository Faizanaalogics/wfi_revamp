Welcome to WorkForce Institute - Where Talent Meets Opportunity!

At Workforce Institute, we are driven by a passion for workforce development and a commitment to harnessing the power of education and technology to shape the workforce of the future. Founded by Ed Sattar, a visionary in the field of workforce development, and incubated and funded by EdTech Ventures, our company was established in 2020 with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between career seekers, higher education institutions, and employers.

Our vision is simple yet profound: we aim to ensure that our communities are equipped with the digital skills that meet the evolving demands of today's employers. We believe that by connecting talented individuals with the right educational opportunities and career pathways, we can empower them to thrive in the digital age.

Since our inception, we have been on an inspiring journey of growth and innovation. Workforce Institute's journey began with the launch of our pioneering program, focusing on emerging Digital Marketing roles. Witnessing the success of this initiative, we expanded our offerings to include UX/UI Design and Product Management, catering to a broader spectrum of career opportunities in the digital landscape.

Our commitment to excellence extends to our partnerships with esteemed institutions. We initially joined forces with Texas A&M and Santa Clara University, setting the stage for the successful launch of our programs. Building on this momentum, Workforce Institute has proudly added HOFSTRA and Northeastern Illinois University to our list of esteemed partners, strengthening our network and reach in the realm of workforce development.

At Workforce Institute, we are not just shaping careers; we are shaping the future. Join us on this exciting journey as we continue to empower individuals with the skills they need to thrive in the digital world. Together, we can build a brighter and more prosperous tomorrow.

Our Mission:

Empower aspiring students by democratizing access to transformative technology education, fostering their success in an ever-evolving digital landscape.
